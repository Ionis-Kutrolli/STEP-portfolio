About Me: Part II

I'm Ionis Kutrolli, but if you are on this page you knew that already. This is my less technical about me page. I was born in Michigan but only lived there for 4 years. After the 4 years in Michigan, I moved to Massachusetts and that is where I have lived ever since.

Hobbies

When I have free time you can find me doing one of three things. The first and probably my favorite hobby is rock climbing, specifically bouldering. I started rock climbing in the beginning of 2019 and it has pretty much dominated my life ever since. My second hobby is CAD and 3D printing. I have always loved designing and creating new things and this hobby falls right into that. It is so nice to be able to think something up, design it and print it into the real world. My last hobby is board games and video games. It always so nice after a long day to play a board game or video game with friends.

Languages

My family is from Albania so when I grew up I learned two languages at the same time. As a result, now I am fluent in both English and Albanian. I think it is really cool to speak Albanian because not that many people speak it in comparison to English.
